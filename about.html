About Us

ONCE A CLIENT, ALWAYS A CLIENT & FRIEND. DRIVE HOME A RELATIONSHIP.

Azuma Realty is the delightful result of a partnership led by Mr. Mohammed Haseeb, CEO, driven by an entrepreneurial spirit and supported by a small yet proficient and dedicated team that has successfully overcome many obstacles, propelling the company to greater heights over successive years.

Being a small team of industry experts, we excel in maintaining clear communication with you, ensuring that the context remains intact without any loss during transmission. This streamlined approach also enables us to facilitate faster and more effective negotiations, ultimately leading to successful outcomes.

As a leading channel partner, Azuma Realty is honored to collaborate with Grade-A real estate builders and developers such as Phoenix, Brigade Group, Prestige Group, Godrej Group, and other reputable associates. Our primary focus is on Grade-A builders, and we are privileged to be associated with esteemed names like Prestige Group, L&T Group, Embassy Group, Mantri Group, Skylark Mansions, Bhartiya City, Sobha, Salarpuria, Puravankara, Brigade Group, Mantri, Nitesh, Concorde Group, Hiranandani, Lodha Group, Lavasa, Rohan Builders, Nyati Group, Total Environment group, Godrej group, and many others.

Under the leadership of Founder Mr. Mohammed Haseeb, Azuma Realty continues to thrive as a result of the close-knit relationship with its small, capable, and dedicated team. Together, they have conquered challenges and achieved remarkable growth over the years.
